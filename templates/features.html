Features

Focus Mode

Focus Mode helps you eliminate distractions by blocking notifications and unnecessary apps. It creates a dedicated workspace where you can concentrate on your tasks without interruptions. Customize your focus sessions to match your workflow and boost productivity. Track your progress and stay motivated with real-time insights. Perfect for students, professionals, and anyone looking to get more done.

This Chatbox

This Chatbox is here to assist you 24/7. Whether you need help with task management, motivation, or just a friendly reminder, the chatbox has got you covered. It provides personalized tips and suggestions to keep you on track. Integrated with Focus Mode and Pomodoro Timer for seamless assistance. Your virtual productivity companion!

Streak Leaderboard

Stay motivated with our Streak Leaderboard System. Track your daily progress and compete with friends to maintain the longest streak. Earn rewards and badges for consistent productivity. Visualize your achievements and climb the leaderboard. A fun and competitive way to stay focused and achieve your goals.

Pomodoro Timer

The Pomodoro Timer is a time management technique that breaks work into intervals, typically 25 minutes long, separated by short breaks. This method helps maintain focus and prevents burnout. Customize your work and break durations to suit your needs. Track your completed Pomodoro sessions and analyze your productivity trends. Ideal for improving concentration and time management.
